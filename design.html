Rationale for Data Driven Art

My website speaks on ways to make money online and the positive impact of the internet. One of the newest phenomenas is the mystery of social media influencing and the rise of content creators. Over the last few years we have seen social media figures live lavish lifestyles,with yachts and jets. Although part of it is lifestyle marketing some of it is genuine wealth. I think a lot of people including myself are always surprised when they find out how much influencers make. People were shocked when it was revealed that a famous beauty influencer Kylie Jenner makes over $1,000,000 per sponsor post. So for my data driven art I want to share a local influencers charge over the last few years, but with an element of mystery or a slow reveal. There will be buttons with years to interact with that slowly reveal some sort of data visualization ,be it a bar that slowly shoots up to reveal the amount. The whole data art page will consist of the content creators content with their videos and pictures placed creatively almost like how a digital album/collage.
